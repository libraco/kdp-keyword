KDP Keyword Organizer

Maximize your Amazon KDP keyword strategy with this organizer. Designed for the KDP Bookshelf's seven keyword fields, each with a 50-character limit, this tool streamlines keyword entry. Simply input your keyword list, and it will intelligently arrange phrases to maximize each field, ensuring optimal keyword placement and efficient use of space.

Step One: Enter Keywords

  • Enter your full list of keywords into the box below.
  • Separate words and phrases with commas (ex: fantasy, red dragons, magic mirror, wizards), or enter each keyword or phrase on a new line.
  • Press the "Organize Keywords" button to fill the boxes located in Step Two. The tool will remove redundant keywords and will display how many characters are still available for each keyword box.
Total Characters Used: 0 (Only numbers, letters and spaces are counted*)

*We treat most punctuation in the keywords and Amazon search queries tend to find the same results with punctuation added vs removed, we strip punctuation from the results in order to allow more actual keywords - if you think certain characters are needed please let us know with an example and we'll investigate.

Step Two: Review Keywords and Copy

  • Keyword phrases you've entered (ex. "alpha male" or "second chance romance") will not be separated.
  • Unnecessary keywords will be removed. If you enter "friend, friend zone", the first "friend" will be removed, leaving only "friend zone".
  • If you have extra space in any of the boxes below, feel free to add more keywords above and click the "Organize Keywords" button again to try and fill in the unused space.
  • Press the "Copy" button that appears below each keyword box to copy the list, then paste it into the whichever keyword field you like in the Amazon KDP Dashboard.
  • Any duplicate keywords or those that couldn't fit into any of the boxes will be displayed as a notice below.
